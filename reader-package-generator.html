How to embed on your LMS
1. Unzip the downloaded package and upload the whole folder to your LMS / web host.
2. Embed it with an iframe:
<iframe src="PATH/index.html" width="100%" height="800" style="border:0"></iframe>
Keep index.html and the assets/ folder together. The document is embedded in the package, so you can also double-click index.html to preview it locally — no server needed.
